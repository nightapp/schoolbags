FunctionDescription
Full Report Creates a report showing all the roll calls of the students in the selected class/year.
Day Report Creates a report showing the attendance in each of the timeslots in the selected day of the students in the selected class.
Day by day Creates a report showing the attendance of the students for the selected class over a peroid of time.
Days absent Creates a report showing the number of days absent in each of the categories of the students in the selected year/class over a peroid of time.
